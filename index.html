About M4i

The Maastricht MultiModal Molecular Imaging Institute (M4i) is dedicated to advancing molecular imaging technologies and applications. Our institute brings together experts in mass spectrometry imaging, electron microscopy, and optical imaging to develop innovative tools for visualizing molecular processes in health and disease.

At M4i, we focus on translating fundamental imaging research into clinical applications, with a particular emphasis on cancer, cardiovascular disease, and neurodegenerative disorders. Our interdisciplinary team collaborates with clinical partners to bridge the gap between research and patient care.

Founded as part of Maastricht University, M4i is committed to education and training the next generation of imaging scientists through various academic programs and workshops.
